UTA – University of Technology and Arts
in Warsaw

We are pleased to announce that our institution, previously known as the ‘University of Ecology and Management in Warsaw’ (abbreviated as ‘WSEiZ’), now officially operates under a new name: ‘University of Technology and Arts in Applied Sciences’ (abbreviated as “UTA”). This decision has been approved by the Ministry of Education and Science and symbolizes the evolution of our institution, confirming the high standards we uphold.

Moreover, this year holds special significance for us – we are celebrating the 30th anniversary of our institution. Over the past three decades, we have built a solid foundation, and now we are entering a new chapter to better respond to the challenges of the modern world.

The new name, ‘University of Technology and Arts’ emphasizes our unique character, where we combine technical disciplines with the arts. We believe this harmonious fusion creates a creative and innovative environment that prepares future leaders in both fields.
